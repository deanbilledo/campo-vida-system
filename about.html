About Campo Vida

More than just an organic food company, we're a bridge connecting passionate local farmers with families who value fresh, healthy, and sustainably grown produce.

Our Story

Campo Vida began with a simple vision: to bring the freshest, most sustainable produce from the mountains of Baguio directly to Filipino families. Founded in 2020, we started as a small collective of passionate farmers and food advocates who believed that everyone deserves access to truly fresh, organic food.

Our journey began in the heart of Baguio, where the cool mountain climate and rich soil create perfect conditions for organic farming. We work directly with farming families who share our commitment to environmental stewardship and sustainable agriculture practices.

Our Mission

To provide Filipino families with the freshest organic produce while supporting local farmers through fair trade partnerships and sustainable farming practices that protect our environment for future generations.

Our Vision

A Philippines where every family has access to fresh, affordable organic produce, and where sustainable farming practices are the standard, not the exception.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we've been making a positive impact on farmers, families, and the environment.
